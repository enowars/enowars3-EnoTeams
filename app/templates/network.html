Network IPs

The translation formula from team-id to IP is:
TeamIp(X): X -> 10. ( X // 256 ) . ( X % 256 ) .1

  • 10.0.1.1 team1.enowars.com
  • 10.0.2.1 team2.enowars.com
  • ...
  • 10.1.255.1 team511.enowars.com
  • 10.2.0.1 team512.enowars.com

Some important IPs that you might need:

  • 10.0.0.2:1337 - The Flagbot where you can submit flags to on port 1337.
  • 10.0.0.1 - The game Router you will use for routing.
  • 10.0.0.2:80 - The current score for every team participating the ctf

VPN

The whole competition will take place within a VPN. It will be based on OpenVPN 2.4. and use IPv4 to connect to our OpenVPN instances. You can download your team's VPN configuration after signing up on this website. Keep in mind that the traffic between teams will only be possible after the first hour of the competition has passed.
